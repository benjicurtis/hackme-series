The Impacts of Tech on the Environment

"We want to educate people on the impact of tech on the environment"

This is our mission statement. We want to spread this message to people to spread our campaign to stop E-waste
The Problem: The mining for the resources to make electronics effects the environment because it costs a lot of earth resources. It also causes ground water contamination.
The Solution: We are going to educate people so they are more conscious about buying unnecessary tech. We belive this will work because they will be educated about the problems caused.
